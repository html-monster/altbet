About

Alt.bet Exchange – Revolutionizing Order And Efficiency In The In-Play Prediction Markets

Alt.bet was founded with the purpose to ensure a completely transparent, neutral, and non-manipulated marketplace for prediction industry participants.

Alt.bet is the first fully digital marketplace in which bookmakers, liquidity providers and retail bettors can sell, buy, or hedge their positions on a fully disclosed and transparent market. The core function of the marketplace is to ensure fair and orderly transactions, as well as efficient dissemination of price information for any prediction bought and sold on the Alt.bet.

Through our four main exchanges, Alt.bet offers unique Price per Unit (PPU) probability pricing approach and pools of liquidity, providing customer’s choice when executing their trading strategies.
